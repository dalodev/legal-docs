Delete your Artifex account

You can delete your account and all your personal data directly from the app. Below we explain how to do it and what happens to your data.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. You will not be able to recover your account or data once you confirm the deletion.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Artifex app and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to your Profile by tapping the profile icon in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the profile screen.
  4. Tap the red “Delete Account” button.
  5. A confirmation dialog will appear warning you that the action is irreversible.
  6. Tap “Delete” to confirm. Your account will be deleted immediately and you will be signed out.

If you have active orders (in progress, shipped, or pending delivery), you must complete or cancel them before you can delete your account.

Data that is deleted

Deleted immediately

  • Name and username
  • Email address
  • Password
  • Profile photo
  • Contact information (email, WhatsApp)
  • Biography and workshop location
  • Workshop name and specialties
  • Push notification tokens
  • Session tokens (JWT)
  • Coin balance
  • Reputation score

Retained for legal compliance

  • Order history: 5 years from order completion (tax and commercial obligations under Art. 30 of the Spanish Commercial Code).
  • Consent records: 5 years (GDPR accountability principle).
  • Subscription data: Managed by RevenueCat and Google Play / Apple App Store according to their retention policies.

This data is retained in anonymized form (not linked to your identity) and is automatically deleted once the legal retention periods expire.

If you are a seller with published products

If your account is a Seller account, deleting it will automatically deactivate all your published products. They will no longer be visible on the Platform.
