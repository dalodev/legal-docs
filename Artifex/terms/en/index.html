The Platform is not a party to any commercial transactions between sellers and buyers. Artifex does not process payments, does not set prices, does not manage shipping, and does not store products. Its sole function is to facilitate communication and product discovery between the parties.

6. Platform Operation

The Platform allows buyers to:

The Platform does not guarantee the availability of any product, nor the acceptance of any order by a seller.

7. Order Process

The order process on Artifex follows these stages:

  1. Creation: The buyer submits an order request to the seller, specifying the desired product and, where applicable, customisation details.
  2. Acceptance or rejection: The seller reviews the request and decides whether to accept or reject it. Sellers are under no obligation to accept any order.
  3. Order statuses: Orders progress through various statuses (pending, accepted, in production, shipped, completed, cancelled) which are displayed on the Platform for both parties’ reference.
  4. Payment: Payment is made outside the Platform, directly between buyer and seller, through whatever means both parties agree upon.

Important: Artifex does not participate in the payment process. Payment terms, deadlines, and methods are the sole responsibility of the parties involved in the transaction.

11. Custom Products and Right of Withdrawal

Pursuant to Article 103(c) of Royal Legislative Decree 1/2007 (LGDCU), the right of withdrawal shall not apply to contracts for the supply of goods made to the consumer’s specifications or clearly personalised.

Given that a substantial portion of the products offered through Artifex are handcrafted and custom-made, buyers should be aware that:

  • Products made to order or customised are not subject to the right of withdrawal under applicable law.
  • Before confirming an order for a custom product, the buyer must ensure that the specifications provided are correct and complete.
  • For products that are not custom-made, the general withdrawal regime under the LGDCU shall apply, although the contractual relationship is exclusively between buyer and seller, without any involvement of the Platform.
